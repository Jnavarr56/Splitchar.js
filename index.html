How to use


<h1 class="splitchar">Splitchar</h1>
<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="css/splitchar.css">
<script src="http://code.jquery.com/jquery-latest.min.js"></script>

$(document).ready(function(){
    $(".splitchar").splitchar();
});

	.splitchar:before {color: #bdc3c7 !important;}
	.splitchar {color: #ecf0f1 !important;}

Of course, you can do more than colour with splitchar. It's up to you :)
